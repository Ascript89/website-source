About me
2015

Gets his first tablet, and starts interesting about Android and tech things and games such as Angry Birds, Real Racing, Minecraft and Geometry Dash.

2018

Downloads Baldi's Basics for PC, and starts playing mods of the game. Starts playing on computer.

2019

Gets his first phone and plays Geometry Dash after 3 years of inactivity. He also starts his YouTube channel just to upload some gameplays of GD.

He joins some other social media like Discord and Twitter.

2020

Some months before lockdown due to Covid, he discovers many GD-related communities, including GDPSes. Around March he creates his account on XGDPS, after liking the owner of the GDPS, and a month after, he joines WGDPS, where he would highlight in the community around GDPSes (not really highlight but starts being known in the community).

In the summer, he creates many levels in WGDPS and develop some software tools for it, and using the excuse of GDPS modding to start developing some programs and websites, and also learning about how is GD in depth. However, he would drop off .NET apps development for Windows, as he didn't really know any compiled language to develop good worthy apps.

Getting to the end of his activity at WGDPS, he starts his own independent project codenamed "GDPS Public Marketplace", and calling it GDPS Hub afterwards. Starts getting quite a good level on front-end development.

2021

LBlazen joins the developing team for GDPS Hub, and build a new notable community in GDPSes. They start releasing the first beta builds after rewriting 3 times the website. His level on front-end development improves a lot.

He also leaves every GDPS forever, except WGDPS just because of all the effort and good memories he has from there.

Present

He's developing and designing for GDPS Hub, while focusing more on his own things rather than playing GDPSes, although his projects are related to them.
